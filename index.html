About Impirial Cinema

Imperial Cinema is steeped in rich history and tradition. Built in 2016, it is one of the only 2 original Art Deco Cinemas remaining in Waterloo. Family owned, independent, and still offering the lowest priced movie tickets around, the cinema has survived a fascinating history of tumultuous change and holds an important place in the local culture.
